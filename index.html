Mental Health Awareness and Support Services

What is Mental Health? - Mental health is a state of mental well-being that enables people to cope with the stresses of life, realize their abilities, learn well and work well, and contribute to their community.

Consequences of Mental HealthUntreated mental health conditions can result in unnecessary disability, unemployment, abuse, inappropriate incarnation, and suicide, and poor quality of life.

Mental Health problems can affect many areas students lives, reducing their quality life, physical health, and negatively impacting relationships with friends and family members.
